Help & Instructions

How to Use

  1. Select the type of ad you want to create
  2. Fill in the required details (image/video URL, destination URL, etc.)
  3. Customize the appearance (size, color, position)
  4. Use the crop tool to adjust image positioning
  5. Click "Generate Ad" to create your ad
  6. Copy the embed code or shareable link to use your ad

Ad Types

  • Banner Ad: Standard display ad in various sizes
  • Video Ad: Auto-play video ad (supports YouTube, MP4, Google Drive, Streamable)
  • Social Bar: Floating bar with call-to-action
  • Popup Ad: Appears after a delay or user action
  • Fullscreen Ad: Covers the entire viewport

Video Ad Features

  • Supports Streamable.com for easy video hosting
  • Audio control (muted/unmuted)
  • Automatic ad display below the video when opened via link
  • One-click upload button for Streamable

Tips

  • Use high-quality images for best results
  • Keep video ads short (5-15 seconds recommended)
  • Test your ads on different devices
  • Use the crop tool to perfect image positioning
  • Save your ads for future editing
