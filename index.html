Help & Instructions

Video Ad Features

  • Autoplay with Sound: Videos play automatically with sound (unless muted)
  • Streamable Support: Use videos from Streamable.com with direct embedding
  • YouTube Integration: Supports YouTube videos with autoplay
  • MP4 Videos: Direct MP4 links are supported for custom videos

How to Use

  1. Select the type of ad you want to create
  2. Fill in the required details (image/video URL, destination URL, etc.)
  3. Customize the appearance (size, color, position)
  4. Use the crop tool to adjust image positioning
  5. Click "Generate Ad" to create your ad
  6. Copy the embed code or shareable link to use your ad

Ad Types

  • Banner Ad: Standard display ad in various sizes
  • Video Ad: Auto-play video ad with sound (supports YouTube, MP4, Streamable)
  • Social Bar: Floating bar with call-to-action
  • Popup Ad: Appears after a delay or user action
  • Fullscreen Ad: Covers the entire viewport

Tips

  • Use high-quality images (minimum 72 DPI)
  • Keep video ads short (5-15 seconds recommended)
  • Test your ads on different devices before publishing
  • Use contrasting colors for text and background
  • Save your ads for future editing and reuse
